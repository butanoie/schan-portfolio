## Overview

The White Papers section showcases sample work artifacts that demonstrate product leadership, technical depth, and cross-functional communication skills. Each document type listed below represents a category of deliverable that reflects real experience from 25+ years of product management, UX architecture, and front-end development.

Documents will be displayed as a list (title + description), following the same pattern as the Projects list on the Portfolio/Home page.

---

## Product Strategy & Planning

### Product Roadmap

Strategic roadmap aligning business goals with engineering capacity. Demonstrates feature sequencing, dependency management, and stakeholder alignment across quarters.

### Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Translates customer needs, stakeholder input, and market research into actionable specifications with scope definitions, success metrics, and constraints.

### Product Discovery Brief

Synthesis of customer discovery interviews, opportunity assessments, and problem framing. Shows how user research informs product direction before committing to solutions.

---

## SAFe Agile Artifacts

### Epic

Large strategic initiative with a lean business case, success metrics, and acceptance criteria. Connects business outcomes to development work at the portfolio level.

### Feature

Benefit-driven deliverable decomposed from an Epic. Demonstrates prioritization, scope negotiation, and how features map to user value and business objectives.

### Product Backlog Item (PBI) / User Story

Well-crafted user stories with acceptance criteria, definition of done, and edge case documentation. Shows precision in backlog refinement and sprint-ready preparation.

### Enabler Epic

Technical infrastructure or architecture initiative — such as a cloud cost optimization audit or platform migration — framed with a lean business case and measurable outcomes.

---

## UX & Design

### Interaction Design Specification

Detailed interaction patterns, component behavior, state diagrams, responsive breakpoints, and animation specifications for a product feature or component library.

### Usability Test Plan & Findings

Structured approach to user research: test objectives, participant profiles, task scenarios, findings, and actionable recommendations tied to product backlog items.

### Accessibility Compliance Report

WCAG 2.2 Level AA audit documentation covering automated and manual testing results, remediation priorities, and conformance status for a product or feature.

---

## Technical Documentation

### Architecture Decision Record (ADR)

Documents a key technical decision with context, options considered, trade-offs evaluated, and rationale for the chosen approach. Follows the standard ADR format.

### Front-End Framework Evaluation

Comparative analysis of technology options for a front-end stack, including evaluation criteria, proof-of-concept findings, performance benchmarks, and a recommendation.

### Infrastructure Audit Report

Cloud infrastructure analysis identifying cost optimization opportunities, performance improvements, and architectural recommendations. Modeled on the Azure audit that cut hosting costs by 40%.

---

## Process & Operations

### Onboarding Program Guide

Structured onboarding curriculum for new team members covering product knowledge, tooling, workflows, and domain context. Designed to reduce time-to-productivity.

### QA Automation Strategy

BDD-driven testing approach covering framework selection, test architecture, coverage targets, CI/CD integration, and regression prevention strategy.

### Release Notes / Changelog

Professional communication of shipped features, bug fixes, and known issues for customers and stakeholders. Demonstrates clear, audience-appropriate technical writing.

---

## Cross-Functional Communication

### Stakeholder Presentation Deck

Executive-level communication of product direction, initiative outcomes, or strategic recommendations. Balances data-driven analysis with clear narrative and actionable next steps.

### Request for Proposal (RFP) Response

Articulates product capabilities, compliance certifications, technical architecture, and implementation approach for enterprise procurement contexts.

---

## Implementation Notes

- **Total document types:** 18
- **Display pattern:** Title + description list, consistent with Projects page
- **Content format:** Each white paper will be a downloadable document (PDF or Word) with a summary card on the listing page
- **Priority candidates for first drafts:** SAFe artifacts (Epic, Feature, PBI), PRD, ADR, Infrastructure Audit Report — these map directly to demonstrated experience
